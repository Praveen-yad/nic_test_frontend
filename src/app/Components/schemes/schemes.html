Government Schemes for Farmers

PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

Eligible farmers receive financial assistance of ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments directly into their bank accounts.

  • Benefit: ₹6,000 per year
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
  • Available to eligible farmer families

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme providing protection against crop losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

  • Low premium rates
  • Coverage for major crops
  • Financial support in case of crop damage
